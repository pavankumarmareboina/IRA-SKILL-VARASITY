IRA SKILL VARASITY
EDUCATIONAL & CAREER DEVELOPMENT PLATFORM

IRA Skill Varsity is an educational and career development platform initiated under the leadership of IRA Educational and Welfare Society (est. 2015) and IRA Consultancy and Research Pvt Ltd (est. 2023). With a focus on transforming lives through quality education, practical skills, and employability training, IRA Skill Varsity operates across the educational spectrum—from early childhood education to college-level job readiness programs. IRA bridges the learning-to-employment gap through strategic partnerships with colleges, state governments, and CSR-backed organizations. With a vision of creating accessible, impactful, and practical learning experiences, the organization ensures learners are job-ready, confident, and aligned with industry needs.
